Safari City — Early Journey Analysis

1) Executive Summary

  • Onboarding is strong; biggest friction occurs at L2–L3 and before Episode‑1 completion.
  • Key spend has a small delay after first win; add instant feedback and guided camera.
  • ~3 free boosters over ~10–12 early levels: good when used to teach/rescue, not trivialize.
  • Priorities: smooth L2–L3 difficulty + ad‑continue on 2nd fail; make first key‑spend instant; structure free‑booster grants; add periodic big reveals; ramp monetization post Episode‑1.

2) Early Player Funnel

Stages: Install → Open → Play Tap → Tutorial Complete → L1 Win (First Key) → Spend First Key (Payoff) → Reach L3 → Milestone 1 → Episode‑1 Complete

3) Insights

  • Largest drop: Reach L3 (autonomy begins). Next: Milestone→Episode‑1 (pacing/payoff gap).
  • Small but important gap: L1 win → key spend; fix discoverability/latency.
  • Out‑of‑Moves / “Don’t Give Up” can end sessions if pay‑gated too early.
  • Free boosters should be spaced and purposeful: teach a mechanic or rescue after a fail.

4) Segmentation Map (summary)

  • Beginners: learning loop → tips + clear key‑spend CTA.
  • Stuck/At‑Risk: fail‑streak or spend friction → ad‑continue on 2nd fail; soften next board.
  • Fast/Momentum: quick wins → streaks, skip animations, optional challenges.
  • Booster‑Reliant: heavy booster/continue use → value packs later; teach efficient use.
  • Decorators: many renovations → themes, collections, before/after.

5) Recommendations (with success metrics)

  • Smooth L2–L3 + protect early continues: ad‑continue on 2nd fail (L2–L4); gem pay later. ↑ L2–L3 conversion; ↓ rage‑quits.
  • Make first key‑spend instant: key pop + counter increment; auto‑pan to task. ≥95% spend within 60s.
  • Purposeful free‑booster grants: tutorial, fail‑streak, milestone. Win‑lift with vs without ≤15 pts on L2–L3.
  • Increase payoff meaning: bundle tasks into big reveal every ~5 min; short narrative beats. ↑ Milestone→Episode‑1 conversion.
  • Monetization after Episode‑1: rewarded‑ad continues; starter pack; segment offers. ↑ ARPDAU without early retention loss.
